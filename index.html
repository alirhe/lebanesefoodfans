Our Story

Food to is the ingredient that binds us together

In the heart of the Beirut, a group of Lebanese food enthusiasts came together over shared love for their homeland's culinary treasures. Inspired by their passion, they decided to form the "Lebanese Feast Fan Club," a community devoted to savoring the rich tapestry of Lebanese cuisine. With each gathering, they celebrated not only the food but also the cultural connections it brought, turning strangers into family, one bite at a time. And thus, their delicious journey began, fostering lasting bonds through the love of Lebanese flavors.

Special Recipe

Shawarma

Shawarma, a Middle Eastern culinary delight, is a flavorful masterpiece. Thinly sliced marinated meat, often chicken or beef, is slowly roasted on a vertical rotisserie, resulting in tender, juicy, and perfectly spiced slices. Served in warm pita bread with fresh veggies and tahini sauce, it's a mouthwatering experience that transports taste buds to the bustling streets of the Middle East.

Delicious Recipes

  • Tabbouleh

    Tabbouleh is a Lebanese salad known for its vibrant mix of fresh herbs, grains, and zesty dressing.

  • Hummus

    Creamy chickpea dip with a hint of garlic and lemon, often garnished with olive oil.

  • Falafel

    Deep-fried, crispy balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, seasoned with aromatic spices.

  • Kibbeh

    Savory, spiced meat mixture, often shaped into patties or balls and fried to perfection.

  • Stuffed Grape Leaves (Warak Enab)

    Tender grape leaves wrapped around a flavorful mixture of rice and sometimes ground meat.

  • Shawarma

    Thinly sliced, marinated meat, usually served in flatbread with various toppings and sauces.
