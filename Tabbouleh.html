Tabbouleh Recipe

Tabbouleh

Tabbouleh is a Lebanese salad known for its vibrant mix of fresh herbs, grains, and zesty dressing.

  • Ingredients





    • 1 cup finely chopped fresh parsley

    • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh mint

    • 2 tomatoes, finely diced

    • 1/2 cup finely chopped green onions

    • 1/4 cup fine bulgur wheat, soaked in hot water

    • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

    • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil

    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Home

  • Instructions





    1. In a bowl, combine chopped parsley, mint, tomatoes, and green onions.

    2. Drain and squeeze excess water from soaked bulgur wheat and add it to the bowl.

    3. In a separate bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

    4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Let it sit for 30 minutes before serving.
