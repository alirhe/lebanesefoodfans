Falafel Recipe

Falafel

Creamy chickpea dip with a hint of garlic and lemon, often garnished with olive oil.

  • Ingredients





    • 1 cup dried chickpeas, soaked overnight

    • 1 small onion, roughly chopped

    • 2 cloves garlic

    • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley

    • 1 teaspoon cumin

    • 1 teaspoon coriander

    • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

    • Salt and pepper to taste

    • 1 teaspoon baking powder

    • 4-6 tablespoons flour

    • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Home

  • Instructions





    1. Drain and rinse soaked chickpeas. Place them in a food processor with onion, garlic, parsley, and spices. Process until well mixed but not pureed

    2. Sprinkle in baking powder and 4 tablespoons of flour and pulse until combined. Add more flour if needed to form a dough that can be shaped into balls

    3. Form the mixture into small balls or patties and flatten slightly.

    4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C). Fry the falafel until golden brown, about 4-5 minutes per side.

    5. Drain on paper towels and serve with tahini sauce.
