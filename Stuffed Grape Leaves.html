Stuffed Grape Leaves Recipe

Stuffed Grape Leaves
'Warak Enab'

Tender grape leaves wrapped around a flavorful mixture of rice and sometimes ground meat.

  • Ingredients





    • 1 jar grape leaves, drained and rinsed (or fresh grape leaves if available)

    • 1 cup short-grain rice

    • 1/2 pound ground beef or lamb

    • 1 onion, finely chopped

    • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

    • 2 tablespoons olive oil

    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Home

  • Instructions





    1. In a bowl, combine rice, ground meat, chopped onion, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

    2. Place a grape leaf on a flat surface, vein side up. Remove the stem.

    3. Place a small amount of the rice mixture (about a tablespoon) in the center of the leaf.

    4. Fold the sides of the leaf over the filling, then roll it up tightly.

    5. Repeat with the remaining grape leaves and filling.

    6. Place stuffed grape leaves in a pot, seam side down, in a single layer.

    7. Add enough water to cover the grape leaves, and place a heavy plate or lid on top to keep them in place.

    8. Simmer over low heat for about 30-40 minutes, until the rice is cooked and the grape leaves are tender.
