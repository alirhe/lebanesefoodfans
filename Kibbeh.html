Kibbeh Recipe

Kibbeh

Savory, spiced meat mixture, often shaped into patties or balls and fried to perfection.

  • Ingredients





    • 1 cup fine bulgur wheat

    • 1 pound ground beef or lamb

    • 1 large onion, finely chopped

    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

    • 1 teaspoon ground allspice

    • Olive oil for frying
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Home

  • Instructions





    1. Rinse the bulgur wheat in cold water, then drain and squeeze out excess water.

    2. In a bowl, combine the bulgur wheat, ground meat, chopped onion, cumin, allspice, salt, and pepper.

    3. Knead the mixture until it becomes a smooth, dough-like consistency.

    4. Shape the mixture into small football-shaped or oval patties.

    5. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the kibbeh patties until they are browned and cooked through.
