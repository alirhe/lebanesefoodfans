Shawarma Recipe

Shawarma

Thinly sliced, marinated meat, usually served in flatbread with various toppings and sauces.

  • Ingredients





    • 1 pound boneless chicken or beef, thinly sliced

    • 4 cloves garlic, minced

    • 2 teaspoons ground cumin

    • 2 teaspoons ground coriander

    • 1 teaspoon paprika

    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

    • Salt and pepper to taste

    • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt (for chicken) or tahini (for beef)

    • Pita bread or flatbread

    • Sliced vegetables (tomatoes, cucumbers, onions) and tahini sauce for serving
  • Home

  • Instructions





    1. In a bowl, mix the sliced meat with minced garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.

    2. Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated meat until browned and cooked through.

    3. Warm the pita bread or flatbread.

    4. To assemble, spread a layer of plain yogurt (for chicken) or tahini (for beef) on the bread. Add the cooked meat and sliced vegetables.

    5. Roll up the bread to form a shawarma wrap, and serve.
